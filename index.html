Sublist

YouTube Subscription Organizer

About Sublist

Sublist is a Chrome extension that helps you organize your YouTube subscriptions into custom folders. YouTube doesn't offer a way to sort or categorize hundreds of subscriptions — Sublist solves that.

Features

  • Create custom folders (up to 3 levels deep)
  • Assign channels to folders with one click
  • Drag & drop to reorder channels and folders
  • View your organized list in a YouTube sidebar
  • Automatic dark mode support
  • Syncs across Chrome browsers via your Google account

How It Works

Sublist uses the YouTube Data API (read-only) to retrieve your subscription list. All folder data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage. No data is sent to any external server.
